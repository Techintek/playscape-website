Playscape Features

Everything you need to organize, join, and enjoy sports with your local community.

Core Features

Designed to make sports organization and participation seamless and social.

Game Management

Create & Join Games

Easily organize sports events or join games created by others in your community. Set up all the details and invite friends or open it to the public.

  • Simple game creation with customizable details
  • Choose from multiple sports options
  • Set skill levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
  • Control participant limits
  • Public or invite-only privacy settings
Social

Community & Networking

Connect with other sports enthusiasts, build your network, and make friends in your area who share your athletic interests.

  • In-app messaging system
  • Player profiles with sports preferences and skill levels
  • Game history and participation stats
  • Connect with regular teammates
Personalization

Sports & Interest Tracking

Follow your favorite sports and get notified about new games that match your interests and skill level.

  • Personalized sports interest selection
  • Game recommendations based on your preferences
  • Customizable skill levels for each sport
  • Health summary integration
